Introduction

As you might already know that google is the most used search engine for many people around the world. Most students are using google for their research in many forms such as research paper, articles images and videos. Even though google give you the option to find many stock images and photos, most of the images aren’t really professional and beautiful. This is why we are going to introduce you to our new Project call P-Search.

P-Search is a website where you can find many professional photos, and used freely under our license. Photos in P_search can be download and used for commercial and non-commercial purpose. There’s no permission needed, even though giving credit to the creator would be appreciate.

Why do we create P-Search?

There are multiple reasons to why we come up with this project. Due to our observation on most students, we can see that they mostly find stock image in order to use for their slide presentation and reports by using google search engine. And as you may know, images and photos from google are not nice. Also, you don’t know whether the images you download from google are under any license. Even though, most of the creator won’t sue you for using their photos for study, some will not be happy about it.

Our Vision

This is the reason why we create this project up:

  • Students can use images from our website completely free due to our license with the creator.
  • Increase more opportunity for photographer to share their works.
  • We also think about those who start their work in the creative fields as well as developer.
